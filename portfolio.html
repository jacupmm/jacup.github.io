Creator's Block

I created by my own typeface for this project using the cut-out shapes seen in the logo and stacking them on a 4x4 grid like building blocks. The hypothetical event was a Zoom meeting where students in the Collee of Fine Arts could talk about their creative experiences over quarantine.


World Mental Health Day Calendar

For this project, the forms in the typography are organic and flowing to represent the fluidity and fluctuation of mood. Each spread in the calendar talks about various mental illnesses and how they can be treated.


Pause Poster 1

I combined photographs I took on a walk with notes I took while observing the emotions and scenery around me. The one word hanging over me the whole walk was “motivation.”


Pause Poster 2

I pose the question: How does the world around you change when you take a minute to pause and feel?
