Dream Mass A 'Dream Mass' is a hypothetical event I created and designed as a vision of what the world might look like if dreams had a greater importance in society than they do now. The 'Mass' would be an event where people would go to sleep for a couple hours, potentially dream, and then wake up and be able to socialize and talk about their experiences while they were asleep. During the mass there would be ambient music playing in the background while a projection of various abstract scenes would blend and sway together on the ceiling above them.
