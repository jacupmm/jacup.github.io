Dream Mass Album Covers The Escapist The Escapist is a look into a world that I have created in response to my living situation. As I created this world, I felt myself getting lost within it, crafting landscapes with bright colors but dark skies. The Escapist is titled after the main character of the story who, as the book progresses, self-reflects and discovers how to get back to their home planet and find the joy in their home again. My hope is for this story to help heal those who are lost from their homes and help them discover that there is always a way to get back to that feeling of belonging.
